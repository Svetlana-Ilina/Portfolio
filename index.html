About Me

My name is Svetlana and I'm a dancer, performer, and teacher. I provide online stretching and pilates classes. My teaching expertise spans more than 7 years, and I've worked with many students who have excelled.

I've been dancing and researching diverse ways to movement for 15 years. During that period, I was fascinated with movement mechanics and their interrelationships. My background is wide; I've studied street dancing, contemporary, floor work, ballet, anatomy, and a variety of other physical activities. I continue to deepen my understanding of the body's labor and movement after graduating from the Department of Contemporary Dance at the Samara State Institute of Culture. Currently, I am a student at PERA, Cyprus' School of Performing Arts.

Classes

The classes are split into two parts, the first one is Pilates: ​

  • Musculoskeletal system strengthening (coordination/balance, weight control)
  • Muscle structure strengthening (shoulder joint, back, abs, arms, legs)
  • whole body tone and dynamics

Stretching is the second part:
  • Smooth muscular stretching, beginning at the neck and finishing at the feet.
  • The spine and hip joint are the center of attention (back, splits)
  • Steady breathing and total bodily relaxation

Why Me?

My online classes are held in a friendly atmosphere, where there are two goals:

  • Get a good result
  • Love the process

You will enjoy exercising with me and learn how to keep your body in excellent physical condition.
